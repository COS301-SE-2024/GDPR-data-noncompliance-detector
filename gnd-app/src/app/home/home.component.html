GND - GDPR Non Compliance Detector

The GDPR Data Noncompliance Detector is a software tool designed to identify instances of noncompliance with the General Data Protection Regulation. It analyzes data sets and identifies potential violations of GDPR principles, such as unauthorised data processing, inadequate security measures, or lack of consent.

To get started just upload a document you want to check for any violations or click on the inbox button to check your inbox and downloads
